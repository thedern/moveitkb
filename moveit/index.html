About Me

Bio

My name is Chris Ferguson...

I have dedicated my life to the practice and teaching of physical fitness in order to promote both physical heath and self-confidence in people of all ages. I hold certifications from Functional Movement Systems (FMS), the International Kettlebell and Fitness Federation (IKFF), and the National Academy of Sports Medicine (NASM). I also hold the rank of purple belt in Brazilian Jiu-jitsu and actively train on a daily basis. When not teaching or training I enjoy playing the drums, watching movies, and hanging around at home with my wife Jenny and daughter Rosey.
